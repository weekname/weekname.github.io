The Aadhar card is a very important document. It is used for identification in government and private services and is also used as address proof. Whether you have to apply for any job or avail any government service, the Aadhar card is taken everywhere. If you have lost your Aadhaar card or have got it updated and want to download it, then the method to download it is given below.

You can easily download your Aadhar card from the official website of UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India). Apart from this, you can also download your Aadhar card from mAadhaar, DigiLocker, and the UMANG portal. How to download your Aadhar card is explained in detail below.

Aadhar card Download

Five methods to download an Aadhar card are given below, by following which you can download your Aadhaar.

Aadhaar Card Download Using Aadhaar Number

  • To download an Aadhar card from UIDAI, first of all, you have to visit the official website, https://uidai.gov.in/en .

  • After reaching home, you have to click on My Aadhaar.

  • Now many options will come in front of you; you have to click on Download Aadhaar.

  • After this, the form will open. In this, you have to fill in the Aadhaar number and captcha code.

  • After filling out the form, click on Send OTP.

  • An OTP will be sent to you on the mobile number linked with the Aadhaar card, enter that OTP and verify it.

  • After that, click on Download Aadhaar; after clicking, your Aadhaar card will be downloaded.

Aadhar Card Download from Enrollment ID

  • You should visit the UIDAI portal at https://uidai.gov.in/en.

  • After the website opens; click on My Aadhaar.

  • Then click on Download Aadhaar.

  • A page will open; click on the second option.

  • Now enter the enrollment number, date , time, captcha, and click on Send OTP.

  • Now OTP will come on the mobile number linked to your Aadhar card.

  • Then you will have to verify the OTP.

  • Now, you click on Aadhar Card, Aadhar Card will be downloaded.

Download the Aadhar Card from DigiLocker.

DigiLocker is a government portal on which you will find almost every government document. You can download your Aadhaar card from DigiLocker by following the steps given below.

  • First, you open the official website of DigiLocker: https://www.digilocker.gov.in/.

  • You have to click on Login/Register, then the login page will open; enter your mobile number or Aadhar card number.

  • After logging in, the home page will open; you have to click on Search Documents.

  • You have to write an Aadhar Card in the search box, and it will be searched.

  • Now you have to click on the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). Now, a new page will open, and you have to click on Aadhar Card.

  • After this an OTP will be sent to the mobile number registered with your Aadhar card, enter the OTP and submit.

  • Now you have to click on Issued Document.

  • Now you will get the option to download the Aadhaar card; from there you can download the Aadhaar card.

Download the Aadhar card from UMANG.

  • Visit the UMANG portal at https://web.umang.gov.in/landing/.

  • Now you have to log in by entering your mobile number or email. If you have not created an account then first create your account.

  • Now the home page will open in front of you; after this, you have to click on Services.

  • After clicking, a search box will appear above; type Aadhar card in it and search.

  • Then click on Download Aadhaar. Now a new page will open.

  • In which you have to fill in your Aadhar card and captcha code and click on Send OTP.

  • After this, OTP will be sent to the mobile number linked to your Aadhar card.

  • Now you have to verify by entering OTP. you can download your Aadhaar.

Aadhar Card Update Status Check

If you have updated your DOB, mobile number, name, address, or any other information in your Aadhar card and want to check your Aadhar card update, then you can check your status by following the steps given below.

  • First of all, you should visit the official website, https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/.

  • Now you have to click on Check Enrollment/Update Status option.

  • As soon as you click on it, another page will open in front of you. On that page, you will see the option of Enrolment ID, SRN, or URN.

  • Then you select the Enrolment ID and enter your 14-digit enrolment number, time, date, and captcha and click on the "Submit" button.

  • After this, your status will start appearing on your screen.

What is Aadhar card

The Aadhar card is an important identity card for Indian citizens. The Aadhaar card is issued by the UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India). It comes under the Ministry of Electronics, Information Technology, Ministry of Communications and Information Technology, Government of India. It contains personal information of all the citizens of India, such as name, date of birth, address, father's name, pin code and a unique 12-digit Aadhaar number. And it is used everywhere, including in banks, insurance, government jobs, government schemes, private jobs, etc.

Kind of Aadhaar Card

  • e-Aadhaar Card: e-Aadhaar card is in digital form. You can keep it in your mobile, computer, pen drive, laptop, hard disk drive, etc. Since it is digital, there is no risk of it getting torn or stolen. And it is password-protected.

  • Masked Aadhaar Card: This is a version of your e-Aadhaar in which only the last 4 digits of your Aadhaar number are visible (called Masked Aadhaar Card), which provides an additional layer of privacy.

  • Regular Aadhar card: Displays all the digits of the Aadhar number.

  • Aadhaar PCV Card: It is made of polyvinyl chloride (PVC). It looks like a debit card or credit card. It is sturdy and can be easily carried in a wallet.
